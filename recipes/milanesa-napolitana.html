MILANESA NAPOLITANA RECICPE

Milanesa a la Neapolitana, although it has an Italian name, is a dish originally from the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina, and consists of meat, usually beef, covered in Milanese tomato sauce, ham and cheese. The meat is prepared in a very simple way, first it is battered so that it is crispy, and then it is cooked in the oven so that the cheese melts. Undoubtedly, there is an incredible contrast of textures that will please both adults and children. Although beef is the most common to make this recipe, the truth is that the Neapolitan Milanese can also be made with other meats, such as chicken, loin... It is also possible to modify the ingredients that we put on top to present different dishes each time. time, since the interesting thing about this dish is the technique that combines the batter with the coating and the final cooking in the oven. In any case, it will be a very juicy and delicious meat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large rump steaks cut 1 cm thick and tender (veal escalopes)
  • enough breadcrumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 4 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • Salt ,oregano and black pepper mill
  • Oil to fry

ELABORATION STEP BY STEP

  1. Season the fillets with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  2. Pass the meat through breadcrumbs, through beaten egg and then again through breadcrumbs. Heat the oil (a finger or so of oil) in a frying pan, when it is hot but not smoking (about 170ยบ approx.) fry the milanesas on both sides.
  3. Once fried, pass the milanesas through absorbent paper and then place them in a suitable baking dish, cover them with tomato sauce, then with the ham, mozzarella and cheddar cheese, sprinkle with oregano.

  5. Serve accompanied by French fries.
